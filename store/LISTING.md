## Full description (Google Play, max 4000 chars)

```
The Way is the official companion app for students enrolling in Georgian
universities with The Way — from your first document to your residence permit,
and student life after you arrive.

FOLLOW YOUR JOURNEY
See exactly where your case stands: translations, university approval,
recognition, ministry order, visa and residency — every stage updates live as
your personal advisor moves your file forward.

YOUR ADVISOR, ONE TAP AWAY
Chat directly with the advisor handling your case. Upload documents from your
camera or gallery and get them verified — no email chains, no guessing.

YOUR STUDENT MEMBER CARD
Once you arrive in Georgia, your digital member card activates with a personal
QR code — show it at partner supermarkets, cafés, restaurants, gyms and
delivery services for student discounts.

LIFE IN GEORGIA, DECODED
A survival guide built for new arrivals: live GEL currency converter, SIM cards
and internet, opening a bank account, metro and taxis, halal food, and every
emergency number — with The Way's own line at the top.

NEWS FROM THE WAY
New partners, scholarship windows and important announcements, straight from
our team to your phone.

MADE FOR YOU
• Full Arabic and English — switch with one tap
• Optional PIN App Lock to keep your information private
• Works offline — your saved data is always readable
• Fast, dark, battery-friendly design

The Way has guided 5,000+ students to 40+ universities across Georgia for more
than 15 years. This app is for students with an active The Way account — your
advisor creates your login when your enrollment begins. Not a student yet?
Visit theway.ge or message us on WhatsApp.
```

## Google Play "Data safety" form answers

Data **collected**: Name, Email, Phone, Date of birth, Photos/Documents
(user-uploaded enrollment documents), Messages (in-app chat with the advisor),
Device push token (only if notifications enabled).

- Encrypted in transit: **Yes** (HTTPS everywhere)
- Users can request deletion: **Yes** — via privacy policy contact (accounts are
  created and removed by The Way staff on request)
- Data shared with third parties: **No** (Supabase/Render act as processors,
  not recipients — answer "No" to selling/sharing)
- Purpose: App functionality + Account management

## Apple App Store extras

- **App Privacy labels**: Contact Info (name, email, phone), User Content
  (photos/docs, messages), Identifiers (user ID) — all "linked to you", none
  used for tracking. Tracking: **No**.
- **Demo account for review**: create a real student account before submitting
  (CEO dashboard → Create a student → Quick account) and put its email +
  password in App Review notes, e.g. `applereview@theway.ge`. Reviewers must be
  able to log in and see the journey/chat/card.
- Age rating: 4+.
- Sign in with Apple: **not required** (the app has no third-party/social
  login — accounts are issued by the organization).
